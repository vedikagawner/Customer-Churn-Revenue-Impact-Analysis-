# Customer-Churn-Revenue-Impact-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer subscription data to identify customer churn patterns, measure revenue loss, and generate business insights that can help improve customer retention.

The project uses **SQL** for querying and business KPI calculations and **Python** for data cleaning, analysis, and visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn behavior.
- Calculate important business KPIs.
- Measure revenue loss caused by churn.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Provide business recommendations to reduce churn.

## 📌 Key Insights

- Overall customer churn rate: **28.6%**
- Monthly contract customers showed significantly higher churn than annual contract customers.
- Estimated Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) leakage: **$73.94/month**
- Estimated Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) erosion: **~$2,047**
- Customers on annual plans had much higher retention.
